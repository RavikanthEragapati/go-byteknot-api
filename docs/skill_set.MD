# The five levels of skill

* __Novice:__ Requires clear rules and instructions to perform tasks. They have little to no experience and struggle to understand context.
* __Advanced Beginner:__ Starts to recognize situational elements and can perform some routine tasks independently, but still relies on guidelines for complex issues.
* __Competent:__ Develops a conceptual understanding and can troubleshoot problems on their own. They can work independently and apply knowledge to a wider range of tasks.
* __Proficient:__ Has a deeper understanding and can handle complex challenges. They anticipate issues, use judgment, and can adapt to new situations effectively.
* __Expert:__ Possesses an intuitive grasp of the subject matter, going beyond rules and procedures. They can innovate, solve novel problems, and often mentor others.
